# Recall — Meeting Intelligence for macOS

Recall is a lightweight macOS menu bar app that captures, transcribes, and summarizes your meetings — so you can focus on the conversation, not on taking notes.

## What It Does

Start a recording, stop when you're done, and get a polished AI-generated summary copied to your clipboard. Paste it into Obsidian, Notion, or wherever you keep your notes.

## Key Features

- **Universal audio capture** — Records system audio (Zoom, Teams, Google Meet) and your microphone simultaneously. Works for both remote and in-person meetings.
- **AI-powered transcription & summaries** — Automatically transcribes audio and generates structured meeting summaries at your preferred detail level.
- **Multiple AI providers** — Choose from OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, GitHub Copilot, or run fully offline with Ollama and local models.
- **Infographic generation** — Optionally create visual meeting summaries rendered as native macOS charts.
- **Clipboard-first workflow** — Results are Markdown-formatted and ready to paste anywhere.
- **Session history** — Browse, search, and re-process past recordings at any time.
- **Meeting detection** — Automatically prompts you to record when a video call starts.
- **Privacy-conscious** — Audio stays on your Mac. Use local transcription and summarization for fully offline, no-data-leaves-your-device operation.

## Requirements

- macOS 15 (Sequoia) or later
- At least one AI provider API key — or use Local (Ollama) for fully offline operation

## How It Works

1. Click the menu bar icon and press **Start Recording**.
2. Recall captures system audio and your microphone in the background.
3. When you're done, press **Stop & Process**.
4. Receive a structured summary — copy it to your clipboard with one click.

That's it. No browser extensions, no meeting bot joining your call, no cloud uploads required.
